Delete your TwinName account

Use this page if you already uninstalled the app, or prefer to request deletion by email.

Before you submit: deleting your account permanently removes saved names, preferences, and Credits tied to that Account ID. Unlock can usually be restored later with Google Play Restore purchases on a new anonymous account. Credits cannot be restored after deletion.

How to find your Account ID

  1. Open TwinName
  2. Go to Settings
  3. Copy Account ID

If you already deleted the in-app account or no longer have the Account ID, we may only be able to process your request manually and cannot promise automatic deletion.

Request deletion

Required for faster verification.

We use this only to confirm and follow up on your request.

Or email support directly

Submitting opens your email app with a pre-filled message to 136825480@qq.com. This page collects the request; it does not delete data by itself. After we verify your Account ID, we delete the account data on our servers and aim to reply within 30 days. Without an Account ID, requests are reviewed manually only and are not guaranteed within 30 days.

Still have the app?

The fastest option is Settings → Delete account and data inside TwinName. After that, you can still use this page if you need a follow-up confirmation.
