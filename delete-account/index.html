Delete your TwinName account

Use this page if you already uninstalled the app, or prefer to request deletion by email.

Before you submit: deleting your account permanently removes saved names, preferences, generation history links, and unused Credits tied to that Account ID. Deleting your account does not automatically start a refund. Refund requests that qualify under Google Play policy or applicable law are handled separately.

Unlock can usually be restored later with Google Play Restore purchases on a new TwinName account. Unused Credits are removed with the deleted account and are not restored by Play Restore purchases. If you still need unused Credits, sign in with the same Google-linked account before deleting.

How to find your Account ID

  1. Open TwinName
  2. Go to Settings
  3. Copy Account ID

If you backed up with Google, you can also mention the Google account email you used. If you already deleted the in-app account or no longer have the Account ID, we may only be able to process your request manually and cannot promise completion within 30 days.
