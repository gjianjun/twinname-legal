Data Safety

A plain-language summary for Google Play Data Safety disclosures. Last updated: July 10, 2026

TwinName is for parents and guardians. Full details are in the Privacy Policy.

Data collected

  • App activity: name generations, saved names, chosen status, feedback, and reports
  • Personal info you enter: surname, optional Chinese surname, and naming preferences
  • Account info: anonymous Account ID; if you back up with Google, Google account link data and masked email display
  • App info and performance: operational logs needed to run the service
  • Device or other IDs: account ID, install ID, and integrity signals
  • Purchase history: Unlock and Credits through Google Play; Credits ledger on TwinName servers

Data usage

  • App functionality
  • Account management, backup, merge, and controlled account switch
  • Fraud prevention, security, and compliance
  • Analytics needed to operate quotas and improve name quality

Data sharing

Data may be processed by service providers that power authentication, Google Sign-In, cloud hosting, purchases, ads, and AI explanations. TwinName does not sell user data.

Security practices

  • Data is transmitted in transit using HTTPS
  • Server-side checks validate purchases, ad rewards, and generation quotas
  • App Check / Play Integrity help reduce abuse
  • On-device Chinese text-to-speech does not upload spoken audio to TwinName for pronunciation playback

Purchases note for Play Console

  • Unlock: Google Play Restore purchases
  • Credits: restored by signing into the same Google-linked TwinName account; not via Play Restore purchases

Deletion

Users can delete account data in-app. After uninstall, users can request deletion at Delete Account using their Account ID. Web requests are sent by email and completed on our servers after verification, usually within 30 days. Deletion does not automatically issue a refund; Google Play or legal refunds are handled separately.

Children

TwinName is not directed toward children. The app is intended for adult parents or guardians seeking name inspiration.
