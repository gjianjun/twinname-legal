Data Safety

A plain-language summary for Google Play Data Safety disclosures. Last updated: July 10, 2026

TwinName is for parents and guardians. Full details are in the Privacy Policy.

Data collected

  • App activity: name generations, saved names, feedback, and reports
  • Personal info you enter: surname, optional Chinese surname, and naming preferences
  • App info and performance: crash/diagnostics-style operational logs as needed to run the service
  • Device or other IDs: anonymous account ID, install ID, and integrity signals
  • Purchase history: Unlock and Credits entitlements managed through Google Play

Data usage

  • App functionality
  • Account management
  • Fraud prevention, security, and compliance
  • Analytics needed to operate quotas and improve name quality

Data sharing

Data may be processed by service providers that power authentication, cloud hosting, purchases, ads, and AI explanations. TwinName does not sell user data.

Security practices

  • Data is transmitted in transit using HTTPS
  • Server-side checks validate purchases, ads rewards, and generation quotas
  • App Check / Play Integrity help reduce abuse

Deletion

Users can delete account data in-app. After uninstall, users can request deletion at Delete Account using their Account ID. Web requests are sent by email and completed on our servers after verification, usually within 30 days.

Children

TwinName is not directed toward children. The app is intended for adult parents or guardians seeking name inspiration.
